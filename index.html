About Us

Welcome to Burrito Bandits, Based in Bristol it is our mission to bring authentic Mexican food to festivals all around the UK! We are passionate about sharing the delicious and hearty flavours of Mexican cuisine with our customers, using fresh and locally sourced ingredients whenever possible. At Burrito Bandits we have a diverse menu offering a mix of Burritos, Tacos, Enchiladas and more!
